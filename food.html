Food

Boboji

A spicy cold skewer snack featuring chicken, its offal and different vegetables and meats.

Brown Sugar Sticky Rice Cake

Chinese name is called "Ci Ba", rice cake is deep-fried, and when people eat it, they need to deep the rice cake with soybean flour and brown sugar syrup.

Floating Snow Jasmine Tea

It is a kind of scented tea produced in Emei Mountain, Sichuan. The time to pick flowers is in the afternoon on a sunny day, and has to be picked before the flower bloom, so that the tea leaves are fresh and fragrant.

Hot Pot

A heat source is placed on the dining table, and people put a pot with spicy broth on the top of the heat source, waiting until the broth is boiling so they can put different ingredients which are served beside the pot into the hot stock.

Zhong Dumplings

Small pork dumpings bathed in sweetened soy sauce, chili oli and mashed garlic. It was named after a local street vendor.

Mapo Tofu

Made with medium-firm silken tofu and flavored with fermented bean paste, beef, red-hot roasted chili oil, and also Sichuan peppercorns.
