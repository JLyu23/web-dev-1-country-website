CHENGDU

A city you never want to leave once you set your foot in

Explore

Earliest settlement in Southwest China is a famous cultural city with a recorded history of more than 2,300 years.

As "The Best City to Visit in China" crowned by the UNWTO, Chengdu has received over 200 million visits annually from home and abroad as of now.

Chengdu is the cradle and centre of Sichuan cuisine, one of the four references of Chinese cuisine.
